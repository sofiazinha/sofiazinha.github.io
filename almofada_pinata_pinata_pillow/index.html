piñata pillow :: almofada piñata

May, 06, 2012

Made in 100% alpaca wool, thi new pillow already belong to the home. It was made using the star stitch and needles nº4. On the back I sewn two beautiful buttons sent by the lovely Maria and Carolina from Bola de Tons.

:: Feita em lã 100% alpaca, a nova almofada já pertence à casinha. Usei o ponto de estrela e agulhas 4. Na parte de trás coloquei dois lindos botões enviados pelas carinhosas Maria e Carolina da Bola de Tons.

Now I'm trying to figure out what to make next :)
