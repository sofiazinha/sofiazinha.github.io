happy birthday daddy :: parabéns papai

Apr, 09, 2012

On the 5th my dad made 60 years old. One of the gifts from my talented cousin C. was a cake in the shape of soccer ball, with a red scarf (from Benfica Club) and my Dad to lead. Neither his famous beret was forgotten!

For those interested, the interior was done with two different types of orange cake.

:: No passado dia 5 o meu papai fez uns belos 60 aninhos. A prenda da minha talentosa prima C. foi um bolo em forma de bola de futebol, com um cachecol vermelho (do Benfica) e com o meu papai a liderar. Nem a famosa boina faltou!

Para os interessados, o interior foi feito com dois tipos diferentes de bolo de laranja.
