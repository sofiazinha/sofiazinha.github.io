new pillows :: novas almofadas

Jun, 15, 2013

Following my new duvet cover, I made a couple of new pillow to the couch.

:: Para acompanhar o novo edredão, fiz umas almofadas para o sofá.

The old ones were very dark and were already getting worn from use. These new pillows have brought another life into the living room.

:: As antigas eram muito escuras e já estavam a ficar gastas do uso. Estas novas almofadas vieram trazer outra vida para a sala.

They were made in 100% cotton, in gray and turquoise.

:: Foram feitas em 100% algodão, nas cores cinza e azul turquesa.

I'm considering doing more, but with different colors and cute patterns.

:: Estou a ponderar fazer mais, mas com cores diferentes e com padrões giros.

Hope you enjoy! :)

:: Espero que gostem! :)
