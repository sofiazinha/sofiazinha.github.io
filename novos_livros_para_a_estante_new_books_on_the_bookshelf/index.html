new books on the bookshelf :: novos livros para a estante

Apr, 11, 2012

A while ago, J. offered me The Hitchhicker's Guide to the Galaxy by Douglas Adams. I've seen the movie so long ago that it's going to feel very good to revisit the story again.

Thanks J.
