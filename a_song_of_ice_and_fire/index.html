a song of ice and fire

Jul, 31, 2013

On my birthday, J. gave me the complete original collection of “A Song of Ice and Fire” from George R. R. Martin. It’s 7 books.

:: No meu aniversário o J. ofereceu-me a coleção original completa de “A Song of Ice and Fire” do George R. R. Martin. São 7 livros.

I couldn’t resist in starting devouring the first book, “A Game of Thrones”. I can tell you that it’s even better than the TV show ;)

:: Eu não resisti e comecei logo a devorar o primeiro livro, “A Game of Thrones”. Está a ser muito melhor que a série ;)
